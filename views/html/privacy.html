Privacy Policy

LocalResponse, Inc. (“LocalResponse”, “we”, “our”) offers a digital advertising platform that enables marketers to target advertising based upon a consumer’s approximate location. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and protect information when you visit our website (the “Site”) or encounter the services (the “Services”) we provide to our advertising and publishing clients.

Information Collection and Use

The following is a list of the types of information we may collect and ways in which it is generally used:

  • Public Information. When you sign up for a social networking site or service such as Twitter, you may create a public profile that includes your name, some biographical information and the general area you may be located. You may choose not to share this information publicly by communicating this to the social networking site or service. Similarly, the information contained in Tweets and other social networking communications are generally public. If you want information on how to customize your settings via Twitter, for example,click here. We may collect that information directly or via partners in order to help our clients customize marketing messages to you. For example, if you Tweet that you are at your local Kmart store, our Service may help Kmart mention you via what Twitter users typically refer to as an@mention. The @mention will typically be some kind of commercial message - announcing a discount or special offer. PLEASE NOTE: Data for accessing the internet and social networks such as Twitter over your mobile device, including Tweets you send or receive (whether or not initiated by our Service) may be imposed by your carrier.
  • Website Logs. When visiting the Site, we automatically collect and store some information that you do not actively provide, such as your internet protocol (IP) address, software and hardware attributes such as browser type, the date and time of your visit, and the last website you visited which are used to enhance the services we provide to you.
  • Cookies. We use cookies in connection with the Site and the Service. For the Site, we generally use cookies to help us understand how the Site is being utilized. For the Service, we may use cookies to deliver targeting advertising on websites that you visit. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can change your browser’s settings to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features and services will not function properly if cookies are disabled.
